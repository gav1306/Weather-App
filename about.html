Creating a small weather app can be a fun and educational project for learning web development and working with APIs. Here's an overview of what a small weather app might include and how I built it.

App Features: Current Weather: Display the current temperature, weather conditions, and other relevant information such as humidity, wind speed, and visibility. Search Functionality: Allow users to search for weather information for specific locations, such as cities or zip codes. User-Friendly Interface: Design a user-friendly and visually appealing interface to present weather data.
